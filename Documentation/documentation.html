System's Responsibilites

              
                The systems team at the department of eTech is resonsible for the imaging & maintenance of all A&S lab machines / spaces, as well as the iPad initiative, the building of custom built high end faulty machines and the support of all Arts and Sciences IT tickets that OIT is not responsibile for.
